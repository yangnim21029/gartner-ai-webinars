## 官方描述（原文照錄）

**How to Identify, Fund and Measure High-Value AI Use Cases**

- Identify and prioritize AI use cases using Gartner's structured evaluation criteria
- Apply AI FinOps principles to funding models, cost transparency and value measurement
- Build c-suite alignment around value creation, risk, and investment decisions
- Establish a repeatable process for governing AI economics at scale

AI investment is accelerating, but so is executive pressure to prove impact. CIOs must show measurable outcomes, CFOs must validate financial returns and c-suite leaders must align on where AI creates real business value. In this session, you'll learn Gartner's rigorous, repeatable approach for identifying high-value AI use cases, prioritizing them based on strategic and economic criteria, and applying AI FinOps discipline to fund, track and continuously optimize value delivery across the enterprise.
