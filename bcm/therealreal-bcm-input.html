01Company context

The RealReal is the largest US online marketplace for authenticated pre-owned luxury goods, run mainly on consignment: it takes custody of a consignor's item, then authenticates, photographs, prices, lists, sells and ships it, keeping a commission on each sale rather than taking title. A smaller first-party channel buys items outright. Trust is its moat — every item passes human luxury experts (gemologists, horologists, brand authenticators) backed by proprietary AI, which lets buyers pay a premium for 'pre-owned but guaranteed real'. After years of losses the company reached profitability, posting positive Adjusted EBITDA in every quarter of 2025, and now pushes its Athena AI pipeline through authentication, pricing and listing as a margin lever while leaning back into consignment-supply growth.
