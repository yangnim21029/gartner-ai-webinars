## 官方描述（原文照錄）

**How to Create a Workforce That Can Keep Up With AI**

- Reallocate workforce capacity as AI changes how work gets done — and where ROI is at stake
- Lead emotional adaptation as AI disrupts employees' sense of competence, value and trust
- Redesign work so AI frees people for new value creation, not just efficiency gains or leftover tasks

Executive leaders face three interdependent challenges in building a future-ready workforce: capacity reallocation, emotional adaptation, and work redesign. Each requires deliberate trade-offs — and leadership at the right altitude. Too often, leaders swing between vague vision statements and overly tactical interventions, failing to address the systemic impact AI is having on how work, value creation and trust evolve together.

This webinar equips CIOs and technology executives with practical strategies to guide workforce transformation, make better trade-offs, and direct AI-enabled capacity toward sustainable business value.
