## 官方描述（原文照錄）

**Leading EA in 2026: A Guide for AI-Enabled Transformation**

As organizations accelerate toward AI-driven growth, Enterprise Architecture is at a pivotal moment. Traditional governance-first approaches can no longer keep pace with shifting business priorities, expanding platforms and increasing cost pressures. Heads of EA must redefine their role as strategic value orchestrators who guide AI adoption, shape technology decisions and translate investments into measurable outcomes.

In this complimentary Gartner webinar, a Gartner expert will share how EA leaders can evolve their operating models, influence AI roadmaps and ensure EA becomes a critical connector for enterprise transformation. Attendees will gain practical guidance on how to modernize engagement patterns, demonstrate value and reposition EA as an essential partner in AI-enabled growth.

You will learn:

- How to frame an EA vision that advances growth, resilience and cost optimization
- How to establish measurable value through AI use-case prioritization and portfolio simplification
- How to communicate EA's impact to C-suite stakeholders using clear narratives and KPIs
